Minecraft is a sandbox video game developed by Mojang Studios where players explore, build, and survive in a procedurally generated blocky world.

Characters

Steve

Steve is the default player character wearing a blue shirt and purple pants.

Alex

Alex features orange hair and a green shirt with a slimmer character model.

Tools

Pickaxe

Used for mining ores and stone blocks.

Axe

Best tool for chopping wood and wooden blocks.

Shovel

Used to dig dirt, sand, gravel, and snow.

Hostile Enemies

Zombie

Zombies attack players at night and can burn in sunlight.

Creeper

Creepers silently approach and explode, causing massive damage.

Skeleton

Skeletons use bows to attack players from a distance.

Spider

Spiders can climb walls and attack aggressively at night.
